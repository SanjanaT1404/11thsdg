SDG Goals & Learning Activities

SDG Title Subject Topic Activity
SDG 1: No Poverty Economics Poverty Simulation Experience the challenges of living in poverty by simulating limited resources and unexpected events.
SDG 1: No Poverty Economics Game-Based Learning Play board games or digital games like Monopoly to learn about economic strategies and poverty implications.
SDG 2: Zero Hunger Economics & Biology Food Security and Nutrition Utilize NASA’s MODIS (Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer) data to assess crop health and productivity across various regions, helping students understand factors affecting food security.
SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being Health Science Public Health Issues Investigate health disparities in different communities and propose solutions to improve access to healthcare.
SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being Biology Nutrition Design a balanced meal plan and explore the importance of nutrition in maintaining good health.
SDG 4: Quality Education Education Learning Methods Explore various learning styles and create a project that caters to different learning preferences.
SDG 4: Quality Education Technology Online Learning Develop a tutorial on how to effectively use online learning platforms.
SDG 5: Gender Equality Social Studies Women's Rights Research the history of women's rights movements and create a presentation on key figures.
SDG 5: Gender Equality Gender Studies Gender Stereotypes Conduct a survey on gender stereotypes in media and present the findings.
SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation Environmental Science Water Pollution Analyze the effects of water pollution in local water bodies and propose cleanup strategies.
SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation Civics Water Conservation Develop a community project to promote water conservation techniques.
SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy Physics Renewable Energy Build a simple solar oven and test its effectiveness in cooking food.
SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy Environmental Studies Energy Sources Create a poster that illustrates different renewable energy sources and their benefits.
SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth Economics Job Market Analysis Research local job markets and present the findings on in-demand skills and careers.
SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth Business Studies Entrepreneurship Develop a business plan for a startup that addresses a community need.
SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Technology Innovative Solutions Invent a device that solves a specific problem in your community and present it to the class.
SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Engineering Infrastructure Development Analyze the infrastructure needs in your area and propose improvements.
SDG 10: Reduced Inequality Social Studies Inequality Research Investigate income inequality in your community and propose ways to address it.
SDG 10: Reduced Inequality Politics Social Justice Create a campaign to raise awareness about social inequality issues in your area.
SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Geography Urban Planning Design a model of a sustainable city and present its features to the class.
SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Environmental Science Urban Sustainability Conduct a study on waste management in your community and suggest improvements.
SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production Environmental Studies Sustainable Practices Research the lifecycle of a product and present ways to make it more sustainable.
SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production Economics Consumer Choices Create a campaign to promote sustainable consumer choices in your community.
SDG 13: Climate Action Environmental Science Climate Change Effects Research the local effects of climate change and propose community action plans.
SDG 13: Climate Action Mathematics Statistics Analyze historical temperature data and carbon emissions data by NASA. Students can create graphs to visualize trends over the years and discuss the implications of these trends on climate change.
SDG 13: Climate Action Geography Climate Mitigation Create a quiz using Quizlet
SDG 14: Life Below Water Marine Biology Ocean Conservation Create a multimedia presentation on the importance of preserving marine ecosystems.
SDG 14: Life Below Water Environmental Science Water Pollution Investigate the impact of plastic waste on marine life and propose solutions.
SDG 15: Life on Land Biology Biodiversity Conduct a field study on local ecosystems and present findings on biodiversity.
SDG 15: Life on Land Environmental Studies Conservation Strategies Create a project to promote local wildlife conservation efforts.
SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions Civics Human Rights Research current human rights issues and present your findings to the class.
SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions Political Science Justice Systems Debate the effectiveness of different justice systems around the world.
SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals Business Studies Global Collaboration Plan a mock international partnership project and outline its goals and strategies.
SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals Economics Global Trade Research how global trade affects local economies and present your findings.
